## 1. Introduction

Welcome to GentleParent ("the App"), developed by Inka-Tech Solutions. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### a) Anonymous Authentication Data
GentleParent uses Firebase Authentication (by Google) to sign you in anonymously. No name, email address, or personal identification is required to use the App. An anonymous user ID is generated automatically to enable the App to function.

### b) Messages and Conversations
When you send a message in the App, that message is transmitted to our secure cloud service (Firebase Cloud Functions) and processed by OpenAI's API to generate a response. Messages are not permanently stored on our servers beyond the duration of your session.

### c) Device Storage
Conversation history is saved locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you initiate a conversation.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- To provide AI-generated parenting guidance
- To maintain conversation context within a session
- To improve the reliability and performance of the App

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

## 5. Children's Privacy

GentleParent is designed for **parents and caregivers**, not for children. The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS) and secure cloud infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 7. Data Retention

- Anonymous authentication tokens are managed by Firebase and expire automatically.
- Local conversation history is stored on your device and can be deleted at any time using the in-app "Clear Chat" button.
