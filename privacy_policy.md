## 1. Introduction

Welcome to GentleParent ("the App"), developed by Inka-Tech Solutions. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

---

## 2. About Our AI Companions

GentleParent features AI-powered companion personas, including **Bra K** and **Lesedi**. These are fully artificial intelligence-generated characters, not real people. All responses generated by these companions are produced by AI language models. No human is on the other side of your conversation. By using the App, you acknowledge that you are interacting with AI-generated content.

---

## 3. Information We Collect

### a) Anonymous Authentication Data
GentleParent uses Firebase Authentication (by Google) to sign you in anonymously. No name, email address, or personal identification is required to use the App. An anonymous user ID is generated automatically to enable the App to function.

### b) Messages and Conversations
When you send a message in the App, that message is transmitted to our secure cloud service (Firebase Cloud Functions) and processed by a third-party AI provider to generate a response. Messages are not permanently stored on our servers. Session data is automatically cleared when you close the App or start a new session.

### c) Device Storage
Conversation history is saved locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you initiate a conversation. You can delete this data at any time using the in-app **"Clear Chat"** button.

---

## 4. How We Use Your Information

- To provide AI-generated parenting guidance and companion conversations
- To maintain conversation context within a session
- To improve the reliability and performance of the App

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use your data to track you across other apps or websites.

## 6. Children's Privacy

GentleParent is designed exclusively for parents and caregivers aged 18 and over. The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at inkateck.solutions@gmail.com and we will take steps to delete that information.

---

## 7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure cloud infrastructure via Google Firebase
- No permanent server-side storage of conversation content

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted through the App.

---

## 8. Data Retention

- **Anonymous authentication tokens** are managed by Firebase and expire automatically per Firebase's standard policies.
- **Session conversation data** is not stored on our servers beyond the active session. It is cleared when you close the App.
- **Local conversation history** is stored on your device and can be deleted at any time using the in-app "Clear Chat" button.
