## How to Delete Your Account and Data

GentleParent offers two ways to permanently delete your account and all associated data:

---

## Option 1 — In the App (Instant)

1. Open **GentleParent**
2. Tap the **Settings** icon (top right of the home screen)
3. Scroll down to **Danger Zone**
4. Tap **🗑️ Reset Everything**
5. Confirm when prompted

This **immediately and permanently** deletes:
- Your profile (name, child's name, child's birthday)
- All conversation history (stored locally on your device)
- Your anonymous authentication token (Firebase)
- All app preferences including dark mode and notification settings

---

## Option 2 — Email Request

If you no longer have access to the app, send an email to:

**inkateck.solutions@gmail.com**

**Subject:** Account Deletion Request — GentleParent

Please include:
- The name you used in the app (if any)
- Your device type (Android)
- Approximate date of last use

We will process your request within **7 days**.

---

## What Data Is Deleted vs Retained

| Data Type | Deleted? | Retention Period |
|---|---|---|
| Profile (name, child name, birthday) | ✅ Immediately deleted | None |
| Local conversation history | ✅ Immediately deleted | None |
| Anonymous Firebase authentication token | ✅ Deleted within 30 days | Firebase standard expiry |
| Notification preferences | ✅ Immediately deleted | None |
| Messages sent to OpenAI / Perplexity | ✅ Not stored by us | Per OpenAI & Perplexity policy |

> **Note:** GentleParent does not permanently store your conversations on our servers. Messages are processed in real time and are not retained beyond your active session.
