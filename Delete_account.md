# Delete Your GentleParent Account

To delete your GentleParent account and all associated data:

## Option 1 — In the App (Instant)
1. Open GentleParent
2. Go to **Settings** (top right)
3. Scroll to **Danger Zone**
4. Tap **Reset Everything**

This immediately clears all your data including chat history, 
profile information and authentication tokens.

## Option 2 — Email Request
If you no longer have access to the app, email us at:

**inkateck.solutions@gmail.com**

Subject: **Account Deletion Request**

We will process your request within 7 days.
